Patrick Star

Patrick Star is one of the ten main characters from the animated series Spongebob Squarepants. He is a pink starfish that lives under a rock in the underwater city of Bikini Bottom. Patrick loves to hang out with his best friend, Spongebob Squarepants. Together, they make the most of the day with fun activities and adventures!
